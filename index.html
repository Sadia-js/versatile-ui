BMI Calculator

A BMI calculator is used to assess body weight relative to height and determine if a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight or obese.

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI Weight status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy

Calculate your BMI
